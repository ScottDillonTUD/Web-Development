About Us

Hi! I'm John O'Reilly and I like to consider myself the world's biggest 'petrol head'. I've created this website to showcase my collection of motors and to help bring the automotive community together.

What We Do

We will collect, showcase and advise our community of motor fans with our vast array of motorised vehicles displayed online here.

  • Displaying and advertising different car models
  • Connecting like-minded people in the greater Dublin area
  • Creating an environment for drivers to share their tips and tricks
  • Help centre for DIY repairs and mechanics

Who We Are

We are a community of diesel/petrol heads looking to connect the community of car enthousiasts in the Dublin area.
